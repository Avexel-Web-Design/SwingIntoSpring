About the Event

The "Swing Into Spring" annual jazz concert is our biggest event of the year, showcasing the extraordinary talent of our student musicians from Harbor Springs High School.

This year's performance will be held at Nub's Nob, featuring our talented high school jazz band in an evening of exceptional music.

All proceeds from this event help support band students in grades 6-12, providing valuable resources for their musical education and development.
