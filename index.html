About the Event

The "Swing Into Spring" annual jazz concert is a beloved tradition at Harbor Springs High School, showcasing the extraordinary talent of our student musicians. This year's performance promises to be an unforgettable evening of classic and contemporary jazz favorites.

Under the direction of Mr. James Wilson, the Harbor Springs High School Jazz Band has been preparing an exciting program that highlights both ensemble performances and impressive student solos.

All proceeds from this event will support the Harbor Springs Band Program, helping to fund instrument purchases, competition travel, and music education opportunities for our students.
